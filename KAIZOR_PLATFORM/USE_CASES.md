# Kaizor Platform — Use Cases (Real-world)

This page lists practical use cases where Kaizor delivers value by reducing noise, speeding decisions, and keeping execution governed.

## 1) Cybersecurity operations (Vulnerability workflow)
**Problem:** security teams have scans but struggle to prioritize, track ownership, and produce repeatable reporting.

**Kaizor helps by:**
- turning scan outputs into a manageable findings workflow
- tracking status changes consistently
- producing a repeatable “latest report” view

The CyberSec Trial in this repository demonstrates this track.

## 2) IT operations / internal support
**Problem:** tickets repeat, context is missing, and resolution depends on tribal knowledge.

**Kaizor helps by:**
- summarizing user requests and extracting missing info
- proposing next steps and safe actions
- capturing resolution patterns for reuse

## 3) Customer support (knowledge + response drafting)
**Problem:** support teams waste time searching docs, and answers become inconsistent across agents.

**Kaizor helps by:**
- turning internal knowledge into quick answers and consistent responses
- standardizing common replies while keeping human review
- tracking what customers asked and what worked

## 4) Research and document intelligence
**Problem:** decisions are delayed because inputs are long, scattered, and hard to compare.

**Kaizor helps by:**
- extracting key facts, risks, and action items from documents
- summarizing differences and tradeoffs
- producing repeatable output formats (briefs, summaries, checklists)

## 5) QA and repeatable checks
**Problem:** QA cycles waste time on repetitive runs and ambiguous results.

**Kaizor helps by:**
- guiding a repeatable test workflow
- capturing findings and outcomes in a consistent structure
- highlighting regressions and “what changed”

## 6) Leadership reporting
**Problem:** reporting becomes manual, inconsistent, and delayed.

**Kaizor helps by:**
- generating consistent summaries and status snapshots
- turning operational signals into executive-ready narratives
- keeping a traceable link between claims and evidence
